Friend of Tokyo

One day you wake up and you are a giant dinosaur-like monster. You wander around for a bit, and spot the most beautiful thing you have ever seen in your life: the city of Tokyo. Maybe it will be your friend!
  • Use the red arrows to rush through the city
  • Destroy buildings in groups to multiply your points
  • Toggle your orientation with the compass
  • Don't let any one sector of civilization advance too much
